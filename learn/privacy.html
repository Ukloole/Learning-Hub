Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Ukloole ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the Ukloole Learning Hub platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number when you register, purchase, or contact us.
  • Payment Information: Payments are processed by Paystack. We do not store full card details on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, course progress, and interaction data to improve our services.
  • Communications: Any messages you send us via email or WhatsApp.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver purchased products and services
  • Issue and verify certificates
  • Send order confirmations and important service updates
  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our platform and content based on usage patterns
  • Send occasional promotional emails (you may unsubscribe at any time)

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Paystack: For secure payment processing
  • Email Service Providers: To send transactional and marketing emails
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure databases, and access controls to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Cookies

Our website uses session cookies to maintain your login state and cart data. These cookies are temporary and deleted when you close your browser. We do not use third-party tracking cookies without your consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Order records are retained for 7 years for legal and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related enquiries or to exercise your rights, please contact:
